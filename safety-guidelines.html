All Vanquish Toys products are intended for individuals aged 14 and above. Adult supervision is required for users under 18 years of age.

  • NEVER use toy guns in public places or near roads
  • NEVER modify or alter toy guns
  • NEVER use toy guns to threaten or intimidate others
  • NEVER carry toy guns in public without proper containment

2. Age-Appropriate Use

2.1 Minimum Age Requirements

  • 14-17 years: Must be supervised by an adult (18+)
  • 18+ years: Can use products independently
  • Under 14: Not suitable for our products

2.2 Supervision Guidelines

When supervising younger users, adults should:

  • Ensure all safety rules are followed
  • Monitor play sessions
  • Provide guidance on responsible use
  • Store products securely when not in use

3. Safe Usage Areas

3.1 Approved Locations

  • Private property with owner's permission
  • Designated play areas and fields
  • Indoor facilities specifically designed for tactical play
  • Organized events with proper safety measures

3.2 Prohibited Areas

  • Public streets, parks, and sidewalks
  • Schools and educational facilities
  • Shopping centers and retail areas
  • Near government buildings or sensitive areas
  • Residential areas where others might be alarmed

4. Product Safety

4.1 Before Use

  • ‚úì Read all instructions and safety warnings
  • ‚úì Inspect product for damage before each use
  • ‚úì Ensure proper eye protection is worn
  • ‚úì Check that the area is clear of bystanders

4.2 During Use

  • Always treat toy guns as if they could cause harm
  • Keep fingers off triggers until ready to use
  • Be aware of your surroundings at all times
  • Use only in designated safe areas
  • Follow all manufacturer instructions

4.3 After Use

  • Store products securely and out of reach of children
  • Clean and maintain products according to instructions
  • Check for any damage that may have occurred
  • Report any safety concerns immediately

5. Storage and Maintenance

5.1 Secure Storage

  • Store in a locked cabinet or secure location
  • Keep out of reach of children under 14
  • Store in a dry, cool environment
  • Separate from ammunition or accessories when not in use

5.2 Maintenance

  • Regular cleaning as per manufacturer instructions
  • Inspect for wear and damage regularly
  • Replace damaged parts immediately
  • Do not attempt repairs unless qualified

6. Legal Considerations

6.1 UK Law Compliance

  • Comply with all local laws and regulations
  • Do not use in a manner that could be considered threatening
  • Respect private property rights
  • Follow any local restrictions on toy gun use

6.2 Public Perception

Important: Even though our products are toys, they may appear realistic to others. Always be considerate of how your use might affect those around you.

7. Emergency Procedures

7.1 If Someone is Injured

  • Stop all activity immediately
  • Assess the situation and provide first aid if needed
  • Contact emergency services if necessary
  • Document the incident for safety review

7.2 If Product Malfunctions

  • Stop using the product immediately
  • Do not attempt to force or repair
  • Contact customer support for assistance
  • Do not use until properly repaired or replaced

8. Responsible Ownership

8.1 Setting a Good Example

  • Demonstrate safe handling practices
  • Educate others about proper use
  • Respect the concerns of others
  • Promote responsible toy gun culture

8.2 Community Responsibility

  • Be aware of how your actions affect others
  • Respect noise ordinances and local regulations
  • Consider the impact on neighbors and community
  • Report unsafe behavior by others

9. Safety Equipment

9.1 Recommended Protection

  • Eye Protection: Safety glasses or goggles
  • Face Protection: Masks or face shields when appropriate
  • Body Protection: Appropriate clothing for the activity
  • Hearing Protection: Ear protection if using loud products

10. Contact Information

If you have safety concerns or questions:

  • Email: safety@vanquishtoys.com
  • Emergency: Contact local emergency services
  • Product Issues: Contact our customer support
